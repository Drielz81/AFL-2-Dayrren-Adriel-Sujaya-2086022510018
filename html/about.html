Our Journey

From a Small Idea to a Big Community

3Games started in 2026 as a passion project by a gamer who were frustrated with how fragmented and paywalled the browser gaming world had become.

He wanted one place — clean, fast, and free — where anyone could jump into a game without signing up, downloading anything, or spending a single cent.

Today, 3Games hosts hundreds of titles across dozens of genres, serving millions of players every month. And He's just getting started.
